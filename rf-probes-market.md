<p><strong>The RF Probes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Type</li><li>Active Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF probes market is primarily divided into passive and active types. Passive RF probes do not have internal amplification; they rely on the external equipment for signal processing, making them simpler and cost-effective solutions for measuring frequency signals in various applications. In contrast, active RF probes incorporate amplifiers to enhance signal strength, enabling more precise measurements, especially in low-signal environments. Both types are crucial for RF testing and development across telecommunications, electronics, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
