<p><p>The walk-in tub market is divided into embedded and independent types. Embedded walk-in tubs are integrated into existing bathroom designs, often requiring structural modifications and customization to fit seamlessly into the space. They cater to those seeking a blend of safety and aesthetics. In contrast, independent walk-in tubs are standalone units that can be installed without extensive renovations, appealing to customers who prioritize ease of installation and immediate access. Both types enhance safety and accessibility for users with mobility challenges.</p></p>
