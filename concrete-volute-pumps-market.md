Single-stage volute pumps consist of a single impeller, ideal for lower flow rates and simpler systems. They are known for their efficiency and ease of maintenance. In contrast, multistage volute pumps feature multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure capabilities and larger flow volumes, making them suitable for more demanding applications.
