<p><p>The laser mirror substrate market is segmented into flat, convex, and concave categories, each serving distinct optical applications. Flat mirrors provide precise reflection without distortion, ideal for beam steering and alignment. Convex mirrors expand the field of view and are commonly used in security and surveillance systems. Concave mirrors focus light to a point, making them essential for high-intensity applications like laser cutting and imaging systems. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing laser performance across various industries.</p></p>
