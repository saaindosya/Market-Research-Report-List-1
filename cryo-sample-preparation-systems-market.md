<p><ul><li>"Plunge Freezing"</li><li>"High Pressure Freezing"</li><li>"Focused Ion Beam Milling"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryo-sample preparation systems include various market types that facilitate the preservation of biological samples at extremely low temperatures. Plunge freezing rapidly immerses samples in cryogenic liquids, achieving minimal ice crystal formation, essential for imaging. High pressure freezing subjects samples to extreme pressure before cooling, further limiting ice artifacts and preserving structural integrity. Focused ion beam milling utilizes ion beams to precisely mill samples at cryogenic temperatures, enabling high-resolution imaging and analysis. Each method enhances sample quality for advanced microscopy techniques.</p></p>
