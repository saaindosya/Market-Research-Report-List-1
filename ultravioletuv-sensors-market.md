<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) sensors market is divided into three main types: UVA, UVB, and others. UVA sensors detect long-wave UV radiation, which is primarily associated with skin aging and can penetrate deeper into the skin. UVB sensors measure medium-wave UV radiation, responsible for sunburn and skin cancer risk, primarily affecting the outer skin layers. The "others" category includes sensors for UVC radiation, used in germicidal applications, as well as specialized sensors for industrial and scientific uses.</p></p>
