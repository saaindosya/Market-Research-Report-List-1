<p><p>In the marine market, composites are categorized into several types based on their reinforcement materials. Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRPs) are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for boat hulls and decks. Polymer Fiber Reinforced Plastics (PFRPs) offer versatility and can be tailored for specific applications. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Plastics (CFRPs) provide exceptional strength-to-weight ratios, enhancing performance in high-speed vessels. Other composites may include aramid fiber or natural fiber options, expanding the range of applications and benefits in marine environments.</p></p>
