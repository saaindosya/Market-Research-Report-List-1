<p><p>Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) encompasses various market types, including LANaaS (LAN-as-a-Service) and WANaaS (WAN-as-a-Service). LANaaS provides organizations with on-demand local area network resources, enabling flexible connections and reducing the need for extensive on-site infrastructure. Conversely, WANaaS offers wide area network capabilities, facilitating seamless connectivity across multiple locations, enhancing scalability, and optimizing bandwidth usage. Both models empower businesses to adapt network resources based on demand, improve operational efficiency, and reduce costs associated with traditional network management.</p></p>
