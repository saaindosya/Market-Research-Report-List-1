<p><p>The water-based spray glue market comprises two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are user-friendly, requiring no mixing, and are ideal for quick applications across various surfaces. In contrast, two-component adhesives involve a combination of resin and hardener, enabling stronger bonds and greater resistance to environmental factors. While one-component adhesives cater to convenience and speed, two-component options are preferred for demanding applications needing durability and enhanced performance, making both types essential for diverse industrial and consumer uses.</p></p>
