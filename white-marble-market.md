<p><p>The white marble market comprises two main types: natural and artificial marble. Natural marble is a premium product, sourced from quarries, characterized by unique veining and patterns, making each piece distinct. It's prized for its aesthetic appeal and durability in luxury applications. On the other hand, artificial marble, crafted from a blend of resins and stone particles, offers a cost-effective and versatile alternative. It mimics natural stone's appearance while providing a range of colors and textures, appealing to budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
