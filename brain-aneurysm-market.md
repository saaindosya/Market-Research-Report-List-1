<p><p>The brain aneurysm market is primarily categorized into four types based on treatment approaches. Surgical clipping involves open surgery to secure the aneurysm with a clip, preventing blood flow. Endovascular coiling utilizes a catheter to insert coils into the aneurysm, promoting clotting and reducing size. Flow diverters are stents that redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, allowing it to heal over time. Other methods may include medical management and newer experimental therapies, broadening treatment options for patients.</p></p>
