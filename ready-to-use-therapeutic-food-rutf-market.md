<p><p>The Ready to Use Therapeutic Food (RUTF) market comprises three main types: solid, paste, and drinkable formats. Solid RUTF is typically granulated or biscuit-like, designed for easy consumption, especially in emergency situations. Paste RUTF, often packaged in sachets, provides a nutrient-dense blend suitable for malnourished children, facilitating direct intake. Drinkable RUTF offers a liquid nutrition solution, appealing for those who may struggle with solid foods. Each type serves distinct needs in addressing malnutrition effectively.</p></p>
