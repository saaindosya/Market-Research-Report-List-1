<p><p>Fire resistant intumescent coatings are categorized by their application methods: spray, brush, and roller. Spray application offers quick coverage and is ideal for complex shapes, ensuring an even coat on surfaces. Brush application allows for precise control, making it suitable for detailed work and touch-ups. Roller application is efficient for large, flat surfaces, providing a uniform finish and faster application than brushing. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to different project requirements in fire protection solutions.</p></p>
