<p><p>Portable X-ray equipment used for security purposes includes two primary market types: Computer Radiography (CR) imaging and Digital Radiography Flat Panel systems. CR imaging utilizes storage phosphor plates to capture images, offering a convenient, film-free solution that can be quickly processed for immediate analysis. On the other hand, Digital Radiography Flat Panel systems employ digital detectors for direct image acquisition, providing superior image quality and faster processing times. Both types enhance security screening by allowing for quick and efficient inspections of bags and cargo.</p></p>
