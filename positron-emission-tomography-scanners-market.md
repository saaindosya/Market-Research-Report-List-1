<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanners market is categorized into full ring and partial ring PET scanners. Full ring PET scanners feature a complete circular arrangement of detectors, providing enhanced image quality and uniform sensitivity, making them ideal for detailed imaging. In contrast, partial ring PET scanners have an incomplete detector arrangement, which can be more cost-effective and suitable for certain applications, though they may present limitations in image resolution and coverage. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs and budgets.</p></p>
