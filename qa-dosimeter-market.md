<p><p>The QA dosimeter market is segmented into TLD dosimeters, OSL dosimeters, and other types. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) use materials that emit light when heated, revealing radiation exposure levels. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters use light stimulation to measure radiation dose. Other dosimeters may include electronic devices and film badges. Each type offers unique benefits in radiation monitoring, used in medical, industrial, and research applications to ensure safety and compliance with exposure regulations.</p></p>
