<p><p>Lathe machines are essential tools in manufacturing, categorized primarily into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical lathes, also known as vertical turning centers, feature a vertically oriented spindle, allowing for the machining of large, heavy components efficiently. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, horizontal lathes have a horizontally positioned spindle and are versatile for smaller, precise tasks. These machines are widely used for producing cylindrical parts and are favored for their ease of use and adaptability in various industries.</p></p>
