<p><p>The Small Unmanned Underwater Vehicle market comprises two main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are capable of conducting missions independently, utilizing pre-programmed routes for tasks like mapping and environmental monitoring. In contrast, ROVs are tethered to a surface ship, allowing operators to control their movements in real-time for tasks such as inspection, maintenance, and exploration. Both technologies play crucial roles across various industries, including marine research, oil and gas, and defense.</p></p>
