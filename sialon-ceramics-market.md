<p><p>SiAlON ceramics, renowned for their exceptional properties, are primarily categorized into two types: Beta-SiAlON and Alpha-SiAlON. Beta-SiAlON ceramics exhibit superior mechanical characteristics and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications in cutting tools and aerospace components. In contrast, Alpha-SiAlON ceramics demonstrate enhanced resistance to high temperatures and corrosion, often utilized in refractory materials and structural applications.
