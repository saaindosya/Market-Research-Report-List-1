<p><strong>The Infrared Gas Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Path Infrared Gas Analyzers</li><li>Closed-Path Infrared Gas Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared gas analyzers market is divided into two main types: open-path and closed-path analyzers. Open-path infrared gas analyzers measure gas concentrations over a distance by directing an infrared beam between a source and a detector, ideal for monitoring large areas and detecting leaks. Closed-path infrared gas analyzers sample air from a specific point, analyzing gas concentration in a controlled environment, making them suitable for precise measurements in laboratories or industrial applications. Both types serve critical roles in environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
