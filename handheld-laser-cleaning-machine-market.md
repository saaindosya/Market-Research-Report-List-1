<p><p>The Handheld Laser Cleaning Machine market consists of two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse systems. CW Handheld Laser Cleaning Machines produce a constant laser beam, ideal for efficiently removing contaminants from surfaces like rust and paint over larger areas. In contrast, Pulse Handheld Laser Cleaning Machines emit short bursts of high-intensity laser energy, allowing for precise material removal and better control, making them suitable for delicate surfaces or intricate cleaning tasks. Both types cater to different cleaning needs across various industries.</p></p>
