<p><p>The solvent-free epoxy market consists of several types, each serving distinct applications. Water-based epoxies are eco-friendly and suitable for indoor use. Conventional solvent-based types offer strong performance but contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs). High solid solvent types feature a greater resin concentration, reducing VOC emissions. Powder coating types are applied in solid form, creating a durable finish without solvents. Radiation curing types utilize UV or electron beams for rapid curing, enhancing efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
