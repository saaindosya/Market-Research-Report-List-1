<p><p>The amorphous alloy transformers market is primarily categorized into two types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed amorphous alloy transformers utilize insulating oil for cooling and provide high efficiency and thermal performance, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, dry-type amorphous alloy transformers use air or resin for cooling, offering enhanced safety and reduced fire risk, ideal for indoor settings. Both types leverage amorphous alloys for reduced energy losses, improving overall efficiency and sustainability in power distribution.</p></p>
