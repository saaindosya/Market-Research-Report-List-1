<p><p>The heart valve repair and replacement market encompasses various innovative procedures. Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) involves implanting a new valve via a minimally invasive catheter, primarily for aortic stenosis. Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair (TMVR) focuses on correcting mitral valve prolapse or regurgitation through catheter-based approaches, enhancing patient outcomes without the need for open-heart surgery. Both procedures are gaining traction due to reduced recovery times, lower risks, and growing patient demand for less invasive cardiovascular treatments.</p></p>
