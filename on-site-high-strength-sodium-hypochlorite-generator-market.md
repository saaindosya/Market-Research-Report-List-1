<p><p>The on-site high-strength sodium hypochlorite generator market comprises primarily two types: the brine system and the seawater system. The brine system utilizes concentrated salt solutions to produce sodium hypochlorite, offering a controlled and efficient process, especially for industries located inland. In contrast, the seawater system harnesses the natural salt in seawater, making it suitable for coastal applications and reducing the need for transported materials. Both systems aim to provide a sustainable, on-demand supply of hypochlorite for disinfection and water treatment.</p></p>
