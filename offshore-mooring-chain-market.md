<p><ul><li>StudLink</li><li>StudlessLink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore mooring chains are essential for securing vessels and floating structures in marine environments. The market features two primary types: stud link and studless link chains. Stud link chains incorporate metal studs that create a stronger connection between links, enhancing resistance to wear and flexibility. Conversely, studless link chains offer a smoother design that reduces resistance to water and improves handling, making them ideal for deep-water applications. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring stability and safety in offshore operations.</p></p>
