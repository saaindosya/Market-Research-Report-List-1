<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Passenger Lifts</li><li>Electric Passenger Lifts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger lifts market comprises primarily two types: hydraulic and electric lifts. Hydraulic passenger lifts operate using a fluid-driven mechanism, ideal for low-rise buildings, offering smooth rides and minimal space requirements for machinery. In contrast, electric passenger lifts utilize an electric motor and pulley system, suited for mid to high-rise structures. These lifts are faster, energy-efficient, and require less maintenance. Both types cater to diverse building needs, enhancing accessibility and convenience for passengers.</p></p>
