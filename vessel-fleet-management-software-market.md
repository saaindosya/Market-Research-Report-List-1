<p><p>Vessel Fleet Management Software is categorized into two main types: onshore-based and vessel-based markets. Onshore-based software operates from land facilities, enabling fleet managers to monitor and manage multiple vessels simultaneously through centralized systems. This type emphasizes analytics, scheduling, and maintenance planning. In contrast, vessel-based software is installed directly on ships, allowing real-time data access and decision-making on board. This type focuses on immediate vessel operations, enhancing efficiency and compliance while reducing downtime during voyages.</p></p>
