<p><p>PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride) binders are crucial in battery manufacturing, particularly in lithium-ion cells. Emulsion polymerization produces PVDF dispersions that offer high purity and improved adhesion. This method is widely used due to its ability to control particle size and distribution, leading to enhanced performance. Conversely, suspension polymerization generates PVDF in a solid form with higher molecular weight, providing better mechanical strength and thermal stability for battery applications. Both methods cater to different performance needs in the battery market.</p></p>
