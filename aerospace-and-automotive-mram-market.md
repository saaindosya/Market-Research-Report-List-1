<p><strong>The Aerospace and Automotive MRAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace and automotive MRAM market consists of two primary types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes a magnetic field to switch magnetic states, offering high speed and low power consumption, making it suitable for applications requiring fast data access. In contrast, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) leverages electron spin to change magnetic states, providing higher density and better scalability. Both technologies enable robust performance in critical applications within aerospace and automotive sectors, where reliability and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
