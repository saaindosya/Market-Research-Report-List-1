<p><p>The commercial three-phase UPS system market comprises two primary types: standalone UPS and modular UPS. Standalone UPS units are independent systems designed to provide reliable power protection for critical equipment. They typically have a fixed capacity and are easy to install, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, modular UPS systems consist of multiple, scalable units that can be assembled to meet varying power requirements. This flexibility allows for easier upgrades and maintenance, making them ideal for larger, growing businesses.</p></p>
