<p><ul><li>Thermal Atomic Layer Deposition</li><li>Plasma Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) Coating Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Thermal Atomic Layer Deposition and Plasma Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition. Thermal ALD utilizes controlled thermal processes to deposit thin films layer by layer, resulting in precise thickness and uniform coatings, ideal for semiconductor applications. In contrast, Plasma Enhanced ALD employs plasma to enhance chemical reactions at lower temperatures, facilitating the deposition of materials that are sensitive to heat, making it suitable for a broader range of substrates and applications.</p></p>
