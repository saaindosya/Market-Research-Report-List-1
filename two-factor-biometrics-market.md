<p><p>The Two-Factor Biometrics market includes various security types to enhance authentication. Passwords combine something the user knows with biometrics, while hard tokens use physical devices that generate code for added security. Soft tokens are software-based and authenticate through mobile apps. One-Time Passwords (OTP) are temporary codes sent to users, often paired with biometrics. The biometrics segment utilizes unique physical traits, like fingerprints or facial recognition, to verify identity, making it a critical component in secure access systems.</p></p>
