<p><p>The automotive fasteners market comprises various types, including bolts, nuts, rivets, screws, washers, and others. Bolts are used for heavy-duty connections, while nuts provide a secure fastening for bolts. Rivets are employed in permanent joints, offering high strength and durability. Screws, available in various shapes, are critical for quicker assembly processes. Washers distribute loads and prevent damage to surfaces. Other fasteners encompass clips and spacers, serving specialized roles in securing components throughout the automotive assembly.</p></p>
