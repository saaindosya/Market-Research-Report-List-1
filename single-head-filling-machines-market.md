<p><p>Single head filling machines cater to various market types, including liquid, paste, powder, and granular filling machines. Liquid filling machines are designed for free-flowing liquids, offering precision for beverages or chemicals. Paste filling machines handle thicker, viscous products like sauces or creams. Powder filling machines are ideal for dry substances, ensuring accurate weight and volume measurements. Granular filling machines efficiently dispense small solids, such as grains or sugar. Each type serves specific industry needs, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in packaging processes.</p></p>
