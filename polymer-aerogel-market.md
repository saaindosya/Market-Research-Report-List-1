<p><p>The polymer aerogel market is segmented into four main types: blanket, particle, panel, and monolith. Blanket aerogels are lightweight, flexible materials used for insulation in various applications. Particle aerogels consist of small granules and are ideal for applications requiring lightweight and thermal management. Panel aerogels offer structural integrity and are utilized in building materials and industrial insulation. Monolith aerogels are solid blocks providing uniform thermal properties, often used in specialized applications requiring high performance in heat and sound insulation.</p></p>
