<p><p>The energy-efficient low horsepower AC motors market is primarily divided into synchronous and asynchronous types. Synchronous motors operate at constant speed and are more efficient at larger loads, making them ideal for applications requiring precision. In contrast, asynchronous motors, also known as induction motors, are widely utilized due to their simplicity and robustness, operating at variable speeds depending on load conditions. Both types contribute to energy savings and reduced operational costs, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
