<p><ul><li>Cellular</li><li>Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OHV telematics market is divided into two primary types: cellular and satellite. Cellular telematics relies on mobile networks to transmit data, offering real-time tracking and communication through cellular towers, which is ideal for urban areas with existing infrastructure. Satellite telematics uses satellite technology for global coverage, making it particularly advantageous in remote or rugged terrains where cellular signals are weak or nonexistent. Both systems enable enhanced vehicle monitoring, safety, and operational efficiency but differ in connectivity and coverage capabilities.</p></p>
