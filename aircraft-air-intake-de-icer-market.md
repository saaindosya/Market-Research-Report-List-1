<p><p>The aircraft air intake de-icer market includes mechanical de-icing systems, electric pulse anti-icing systems, and electric heating anti-icing systems. Mechanical de-icing systems use moving parts to remove ice buildup through vibration or physical impact. Electric pulse anti-icing systems apply short bursts of electrical energy to prevent ice formation. Electric heating anti-icing systems utilize resistive heating elements to melt ice and maintain airflow. Each type provides effective ice management to ensure optimal engine performance and safety in adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
