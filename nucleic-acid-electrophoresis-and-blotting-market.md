<p><p>Nucleic Acid Electrophoresis and Blotting are essential techniques used in molecular biology for the separation and identification of nucleic acids, such as DNA and RNA. Electrophoresis utilizes an electric field to separate nucleic acids based on size and charge, while blotting techniques, such as Southern or Northern blotting, transfer these separated nucleic acids onto a membrane for further analysis. These methods are critical in various applications, including genetic research, diagnostics, and forensics.</p><p>The Nucleic Acid Electrophoresis and Blotting Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing prevalence of genetic disorders and a rise in research and development activities within biotechnology and pharmaceutical sectors.
