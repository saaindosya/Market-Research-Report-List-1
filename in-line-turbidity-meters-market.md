<p><p>In-line turbidity meters are essential for continuously monitoring water quality. The optical meter utilizes light scattering technology, measuring the intensity of scattered light to determine turbidity levels. It's widely used in various industries due to its reliability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, laser meters employ a laser beam to achieve precise measurements, offering higher sensitivity and accuracy, particularly in low turbidity scenarios. Both types are crucial for applications in municipal water treatment, agriculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
