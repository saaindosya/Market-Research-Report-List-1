<p><strong>The 4-Quadrant Photodiode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PIN Type</li><li>APD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4-Quadrant Photodiode market comprises two main types: PIN and APD. PIN photodiodes utilize a p-type, intrinsic, and n-type semiconductor configuration, offering fast response times and low noise, making them suitable for applications like communication systems. In contrast, Avalanche Photodiodes (APD) operate under a high reverse bias, enabling internal gain and enhanced sensitivity, ideal for low-light applications like fiber optics. Both types serve vital roles in diverse sectors such as telecommunications, medical devices, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
