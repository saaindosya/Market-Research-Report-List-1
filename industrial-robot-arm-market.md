<p><p>The industrial robot arm market is mainly categorized into stand-alone robot arms and collaborative robot arms. Stand-alone robot arms are designed for high-speed, repetitive tasks in isolated environments, often requiring safety enclosures. They excel in heavy-duty manufacturing processes, enhancing productivity and precision. In contrast, collaborative robot arms, or cobots, are built to work alongside human operators, featuring advanced sensors and safety mechanisms to ensure safe interaction. This flexibility enables them to adapt to various tasks without the need for extensive safety barriers.</p></p>
