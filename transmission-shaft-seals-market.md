<p><p>Transmission shaft seals play a critical role in preventing leakage and contamination in various machinery. Radial shaft seals are designed to fit around rotating shafts, offering excellent sealing against fluids. Metal face seals provide robust sealing solutions for high-pressure applications, using metal contact surfaces for durability. V-ring seals are tensioned seals that fit snugly around shafts, accommodating axial movement. Other market types may include labyrinth seals and lip seals, each serving specific functions based on application requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
