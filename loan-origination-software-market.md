<p><p>Loan origination software (LOS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based LOS is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software via the internet, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise LOS is installed locally on a company's hardware, providing greater control over data and customization, but often requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
