<p><p>Thermography cameras (TIC) are essential tools for capturing thermal images, showcasing temperature variations in various applications. The market is primarily segmented into cooled and uncooled TICs. Cooled TICs utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and are ideal for high-performance scenarios, such as scientific research and military applications. In contrast, uncooled TICs operate at ambient temperatures, making them more versatile and cost-effective for general applications like building diagnostics and electrical inspections. Both types cater to diverse user needs in efficiency and performance.</p></p>
