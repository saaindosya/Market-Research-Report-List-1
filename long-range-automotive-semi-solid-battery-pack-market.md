<p><p>The Long-Range Automotive Semi-Solid Battery Pack market features two main types: Oxide and Polymer semi-solid battery packs. Oxide semi-solid batteries utilize metal oxides for the active material, providing high energy density and thermal stability, ideal for long-range applications. In contrast, Polymer semi-solid battery packs utilize polymer-based electrolytes, offering flexibility, weight reduction, and improved safety. Both types aim to enhance electric vehicle performance by delivering higher energy density, faster charging, and increased lifespan compared to traditional battery technologies.</p></p>
